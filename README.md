I'm a backend engineer who can't stop tinkering. During the day I'm writing **Go** and wrangling **Kubernetes** at [**Devtron Labs**](https://github.com/devtron-labs). By night I'm down a rabbit hole — training embeddings, wiring up AI agents, or figuring out why a transformer attends the way it does. I like understanding how things work, whether that's a distributed system or an NLP pipeline. Sometimes I end up caring about the product side too — not because it's my job, but because I can't help asking *"but should we even build this?"*
